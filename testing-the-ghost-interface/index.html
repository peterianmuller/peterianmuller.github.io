Thoughts on the cascade

11 December 2016

I have been reading through various resources on CSS and it's been helpful for me to consider the meaning of the cascade when it comes to CSS.

The cascade deals with the layout of the style rules we encounter in our style pages - both the location of a rule within the stylesheet and the location of a property within a specific rule.
